About Us

Jose and Zular Rodríguez opened Oguis Auto Repair in 2007 and have since been servicing hundreds of customers in the Riverwest neighborhood. Jose takes care of the cars while Zular manages the day to day business operations.

The business is named Oguis as a tribute to Jose’s father in part to pay respect, as well as for personal meaning. The Rodríguezes’ have a close relationship with their community and are dedicated members of St. Robert’s Church. They have two beautiful children who both attend local Catholic schools. They enjoy being a part of the Milwaukee community and find it a great place to raise a family.
